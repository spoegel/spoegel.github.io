Sebastian Pögel

I am a theoretical physicist and postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zurich, Switzerland.
My work is focused on mathematical properties of scattering amplitudes in Quantum Field Theory.
I am particularly interested in Feynman Integrals and their algebraic and geometric properties.

Next

Curriculum Vitae

I obtained my Bachelor and Master in 2016 and 2018 at the RWTH Aachen University in Germany. As part of my PhD, from 2018 to 2021 I was part of the SAGEX Innovative Traning Network funded by the European Union, working at the Institut de Physique Theorique of the CEA Saclay in France. After obtaining my PhD from the Université Paris-Saclay in 2021, I spent three years as a postdoctoral researcher in the THEP group of the Johannes-Gutenberg University in Mainz, Germany. From November 2024 to September 2025, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the theory group of the Paul Scherrer Institute in Villigen, Switzerland. As of October 2025, I am in the Department of Astrophysics of the University of Zurich, Switzerland.
