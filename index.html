Sebastian Pögel

I am a theoretical physicist and postdoctoral researcher at the Johannes-Gutenberg University in Mainz, Germany. My work focuses on mathematical properties of scattering amplitudes, particularly Feynman Integrals.

Next

Curriculum Vitae

I obtained my Bachelor and Master in 2016 and 2018 at the RWTH Aachen University in Germany. As part of my PhD, from 2018 to 2021 I was part of the SAGEX Innovative Traning Network funded by the European Union, working at the Institut de Physique Theorique of the CEA Saclay in France under the supervision of David Kosower. After obtaining my PhD from the Université Paris-Saclay in 2021, I have been working as a postdoctoral researcher in the THEP group of the Johannes-Gutenberg University in Mainz, Germany.
